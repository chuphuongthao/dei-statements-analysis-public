Benedictine College TheEquity in Athletics Disclosure Actis designed to make prospective students aware of a school’s commitment to providing equitable athletic opportunities for its men and women students. Any co-educational institution of higher education that participates in a federal student aid program must prepare an EADA reportby December 31.
