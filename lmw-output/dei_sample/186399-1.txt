Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Rutgers-Newark is committed to making diversity a way of university life, where all members of our community feel welcome, valued and respected. Rutgers-Newark students, faculty and visitors will find resources to learn about diverse communities and their challenges, connect with peers, and receive support We strive to offer diverse opportunities that challenge students’ concepts and open their minds. Rutgers Newark has several interdisciplinary programs that feature study focused on diversity and equity, including Africana Studies, Gender Studies and more. To advocate campus-wide inclusivity, we promote initiatives that support diverse communities and raise awareness of the complex identity issues inherent in the lives of Rutgers-Newark students. Rutgers University–Newark utilized the diversity strategic planning process as an opportunity to revisit and extend the RU–N strategic plan, “Where Opportunity Meets Excellence." Learn more about new and ongoing initiatives that help forward Rutgers–Newark's diversity goals.
