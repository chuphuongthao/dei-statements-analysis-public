Pathways to Equity Wingate University strives to cultivate an inclusive and welcoming environment. The University affirms principles of fairness and social justice. We believe a diverse academy supports greater learning outcomes, enhances the campus ethos, prepares students and graduates for a multicultural workforce and society, and contributes to self-reflection and growth. We will work to be an institution that embraces inclusiveness and champions equity. Dr. Dana PattersonChief Diversity Officer Wingate University expressly prohibits any form of discrimination or harassment based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, national origin, age, genetic information, disability or veteran status.
