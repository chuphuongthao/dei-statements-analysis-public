Our office endeavors to pursue, promote and maintain programs that will heighten the awareness, presence and success of minorities and other under–represented groups at Wingate University. Our goal is to support INCLUSION, enhance DIVERSITY, and provide OPPORTUNITY to  minorities and under-represented groups. As required by Title IX of the Education Amendments Act of 1972, Wingate University does not discriminate on the basis of sex and prohibits sex discrimination in any education program or activity that it operates, including in admission and employment. Sex discrimination includes discrimination based on sex stereotypes, sex characteristics, pregnancy or related conditions, sexual orientation, and gender identity.
